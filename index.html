OUR STORY

Welcome to Cozy Haven Retreat

Established in 2013, Cozy Haven Retreat began as a passion project of the Henderson family. What started as a charming countryside cottage has transformed into an award-winning bed and breakfast known for its warm hospitality and attention to detail.

Our mission is to provide guests with an unforgettable experience that combines the comforts of home with the luxury of a boutique hotel. Every room is thoughtfully designed to create a serene atmosphere where you can truly unwind.
